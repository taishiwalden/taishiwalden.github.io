About me

I'm an undergraduate at the University of Washington in the Henry M. Jackson School of International Studies. I specialized in International Political Economy (IPE), a sub-field of economics and political science, which explores problems that arise from international finance, trade, multinational corporations, along with the consequences of economic globalization.

Over the past year, I've supplemented my academics with rigorous self-education in graphic design, web development, coding, and entreprenuership. I've always had a passion for bringing things to life. From the ideation stage, to researching, planning, and executing, I enjoy building personal projects to learn hands-on. Visneto is my latest endeavor: we're revolutionizing the way people interact with their city through finding vibrant nightlife and new experiences.

When I'm not behind my laptop, you'll find me boxing, reading, hiking, taking photos, or otherwise exploring the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

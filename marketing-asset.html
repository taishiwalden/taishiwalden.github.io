Interactive PDF Marketing Asset

Developed a 25 page Marketing Asset for Visneto Media using Adobe Indesign. The Marketing Asset is deployed using a popup on WordPress allowing users to enter their emails to recieve the FREE Digital Marketing Guide.

The marketing guide covers topics on inbound marketing strategies, fundamentals of search engine optimization, cross channel optimization, and even includes tools and resources on furthering ones ability in the digital marketing landscape. I installed Google Analytics tracking code to measure the effectiveness of the call-to-action (aka downloads).

Tools: Illustrator, InDesign, WordPress, MailChimp, Cross-Channel Marketing.

Date: December 28, 2021

Category: Branding, Graphic design, Art Technology
